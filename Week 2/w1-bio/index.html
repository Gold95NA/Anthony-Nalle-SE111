Bio

My name is Anthony Nalle. I was born in Newport, RI on December 30th 1995. From there I moved to Fall River Massachusetts until I was 5 years old where I then moved back to Newport and stayed until just recently this past August I moved back to Fall River. I'm Married with 2 children. My wife's name is Cheyenne Wheeler and she is a Respiratory Therapist at Rhode Island Hospital. I have 2 boys, my eldest is 5 and is named Levi and I have a 6 month old named Westyn. Currently I'm working at Job Lot while attending classes here at NEIT and look forward to my future here and beyond.

Schooling

For elementary school I bounced around a bit. I attended Underwood Elementary School in Newport RI for Kindergarten. Finally, I attended Rogers High School where I graduated in 2012. As of now I'm studying to achieve my bachelors in software engineering here at NEIT and am enjoying the ride outside of some less the ideal circumstances during this second quarter.
